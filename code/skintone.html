    pinkish white skin

    - Can wear all shades and genre of clothes .

    - Should choose to wear light-colored clothes.

    - Choose a hot tone color.

    - Better to wear Pastel colors, light blue, light pink and cream .

    Tan skin

    - Should wear neutral to cool tones or color is close to skin tone such as brown, khaki, gray ,blue , purple or olive green.

    - Shouldn’t put dark clothes, fresh colors and neon color.

    White yellow skin

    - Can wear dark clothes or light clothes .

    - Better to wear light orange brown light blue dark red .

    - Shouldn’t put dark grey, dark green and beige.

    Honey skin

    - Should wear cool tones, medium, light colors such as Turquoise, pinkish purple, beige, light brown, reddish brown and earth tone .

    - Better to wear light green, carmine red, orange pink .

    - Shouldn’t put warm colors and flashy colors .

    TIPS

    Should

    - Use no more than 3 colors.

    - Should match light shirt with dark pants or skirt and dark shirt + light pants or skirt .

    - Match patterned clothes with medium tones, dark tones .

    - Match colors cream with brown .

    - Use a neutral color such as gray .

    - Match 1 full color with 1 neutral color .

    - Match 1 full color with 2 neutral colors .

    Shouldn’t

    - wearing too many pastel or soft colors .

    - Wear clothes colors that look the same but are not the same such as brown and black .

    - Use opposite colors .

    - Wearing warm tones and cool tones in one set .

    - Match orange shirt + blue pants

    - Match green shirt + yellow pants

    - Match black + dark blue
